# Privacy Policy – FuseHarbor

Last updated: May 2026

FuseHarbor is a home electrification quote request app designed to help homeowners start project requests for services such as EV charger installation, electrical panel upgrades, heat pumps, battery backup, and related home energy improvements.

## Information We Collect

FuseHarbor may collect information that users voluntarily submit through the app or website when requesting a quote or contacting us.

This may include:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Service address or project location
* Project details related to home electrification services
* Notes or information submitted through quote request forms

## How We Use Information

We use the information submitted through FuseHarbor to:

* Review quote requests
* Respond to user inquiries
* Understand project needs
* Coordinate possible next steps
* Improve the app, website, and service experience
* Communicate with users about their submitted request

## Information Sharing

FuseHarbor does **not** sell personal information.

We may share submitted information only when necessary to:

* Respond to a user’s request
* Support business operations
* Work with trusted service providers
* Comply with legal obligations
* Help coordinate potential project-related services

## Payments

FuseHarbor does not collect or store payment card numbers directly in the app.

If payment features are used in the future, payment processing may be handled by a third-party payment provider. FuseHarbor does not directly store full credit card or bank account information.

## Location Information

FuseHarbor does not collect real-time GPS location data.

Users may voluntarily provide a service address or project location as part of a quote request.

## Children’s Privacy

FuseHarbor is not designed for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Permissions

FuseHarbor does not require:

* Camera access
* Microphone access
* Contacts access
* Precise GPS location access
* Photos or videos access

## Third-Party Services

FuseHarbor may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, website, database, hosting, email communication, or payment-related features.

These services may process information only as needed to support FuseHarbor’s functionality.

FuseHarbor does not use personal information for third-party advertising or tracking.

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through FuseHarbor.

However, no online system, app, website, database, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you submitted to FuseHarbor, subject to legal and operational requirements.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
